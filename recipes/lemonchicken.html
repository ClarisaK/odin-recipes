Lemon Chicken with Steamed Vegetables

Succulent chicken breasts marinated in a blend of fresh lemon juice, minced garlic, and olive oil create a refreshing and zesty taste. Paired with a medley of steamed broccoli, carrots, and cauliflower

Ingredients:
  • Chicken breasts (4 units)
  • Lemons (2 units, juice)
  • Garlic (3 cloves, minced)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Steamed vegetables (broccoli, carrots, cauliflower)

Steps:

  1. Marinate the chicken breasts with lemon juice, minced garlic, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Let it rest for at least 30 minutes.

  2. Cook the chicken breasts in a hot pan until golden and fully cooked.
  3. While the chicken is cooking, prepare the steamed vegetables.
  4. Serve the chicken over the steamed vegetables. Ready to enjoy!
