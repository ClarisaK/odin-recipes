Pasta with Tomato Sauce and Meatballs

Al dente pasta coated in a rich tomato sauce, crafted with sautéed onions, garlic, and fresh basil, sets the stage for this timeless dish.

Ingredients:
  • Pasta (400 g)
  • Meatballs (homemade or store-bought, 16 units)
  • Crushed tomatoes (800 g)
  • Onion (1 unit, chopped)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Fresh basil (1/4 cup, chopped)
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (to taste)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:
  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Set aside.
  2. In a large pan, sauté the chopped onion and minced garlic until golden.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes and basil to the pan. Cook over medium heat for 15 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, cook the meatballs in another pan until fully cooked.
  5. Incorporate the meatballs into the tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve the meatballs and sauce over the cooked pasta. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese. Delicious!
